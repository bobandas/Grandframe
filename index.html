About GrandFrame

We create lasting moments with the click of a shutter. We offer full video production services & digital multi-camera live event production. We also offer photography, Digital Albums, Our clients have come to expect only the highest level of expertise from us while staying within their budgets. We truly enjoy getting to know our clients. We feel like the people who are drawn to Our work are always a perfect fit. We love each and every one of Our clients (We also like to call them friends, because that's what they become). We not only love taking photos of people but getting to know them and giving them beautiful images that they can cherish the rest of their lives. We share the same belief that private events are truly special occasions and each has its own story that is waiting to be told. We work with our clients to capture memories that can be retold for years to come through photography and videography. ​ Capturing the right moment is what makes a wedding photography special. From the splendor of the wedding ceremony to the rejoicing at the reception hall, your wedding day is a time wherein every single moment has to be treasured down the memory lane so that you can remember them for a lifetime. One way to go that extra mile and do something special is to give tangible memories of the event. We record the day's emotions and events and create your fairytale wedding story in pictures. It is a special day that deserves the best in photography by an experienced wedding photographer.
